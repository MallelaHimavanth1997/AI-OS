# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and inclusive community.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Welcome diverse perspectives.
- Focus on collaboration and learning.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting
Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers through repository discussions or private contact channels.
